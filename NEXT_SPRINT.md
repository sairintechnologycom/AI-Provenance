# Next Sprint: Partner Scale & Trust Tuning

The focus shifts from "launch readiness" to "operational scale" and "analysis precision".

## Primary Goals
1. **Quality Tuning**: Use developer feedback to reduce false positives in AI detection.
2. **Onboarding Execution**: Scale from first 3 design partners to 10+.
3. **Weekly Digest**: Send a summary of AI usage and risk trends to engineering leads.
4. **Lightweight Proposal Flow**: Track manual sales/contract conversations within the dashboard.
